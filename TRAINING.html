TRAINING CURRICULUM

The GIS Course has been designed to introduce learners to the fascinating world of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and their multidimensional & multipurpose applications. This course serves as a foundational guide to understand, apply and analyze spatial data, with a focus on practical tools like Quantum Geographical Information System (QGIS) which is open source & very efficient to empower users with hands-on experience.There are two modules in this Basic & Advance

Basics GIS Introduction: Understand its basic concepts and Relevance. Coordinate systems – Geographic / Projected.
QGIS Applications – Hands-on
> Introduction to GUI
> Geo-referring concepts
> Digitizing & Editing
> Analysis (Spatial Queries)
> Importing & Exporting CSV / KML
> Map Creation & Layout
> Remote Sensing, GNSS & GIS fundamentals
